How I Work

A simple path from AI interest to useful execution.

The job is not to impress people with possibilities. The job is to reduce confusion, create alignment, and help the business move.

  • Start with the business constraint, economics, and stakeholder reality.
  • Choose one or two high-value use cases instead of chasing ten low-value experiments.
  • Define success metrics, owners, and the next delivery step so momentum survives after the meeting.
